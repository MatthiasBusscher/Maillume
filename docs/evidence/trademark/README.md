## Founder Decision

Matthias Busscher accepts the documented medium residual similarity risk for
the limited beta, approves a 31 August 2026 deadline for professional review,
and approves EUTM-first filing evaluation.

Until that review is complete:

- Maillume is described as an email-risk scanner and phishing-risk assessment
  tool, not an encrypted mailbox, secure email service, or email host.
- No registered-trademark claim or registered-mark symbol may be used.
- A US-focused launch, major paid international campaign, marketplace
  submission, and substantial irreversible branding spend remain on hold.
- BOIP, EUIPO/TMview, WIPO, and materially similar market use are reviewed
  quarterly and before entering a new market or making a major paid-marketing
  commitment.
- A credible objection, evidence of confusion with Mailum, an adverse
  professional opinion, or inability to obtain acceptable protection triggers
  a rename decision.

The intended filing evaluation is an EUTM word mark in Nice classes 9 and 42,
with a narrower Benelux filing retained as an alternative. Final goods and
services wording must be professionally reviewed and must not claim services
that Maillume does not provide.
